White Space and Clean Design

Apple

Apple.com

We can observe a great amount of white space, but a smaller amount of white space is applied between the title and the text above to indicate a relationship between elements. Also, there are few elements competing and white space makes it easier to focus on those elements resulting in a clean and balanced design.

Contrast

Spotify

Spotify.com

Contrast is created by using colors. We can see the difference between foreground and background colors, being the background dark blue and the foreground a bright green which enhance readability.

Repetition

Klientboost

Klientboost.com

We can observe repetition on the buttons to get a free marketing plan. They used red to catch the user's attention to an important information, and this strategy is used multiple times as you scroll through the website. Also the word "marketing" is used repeatedly and the logo is used to catch the attention to the chatbot.
