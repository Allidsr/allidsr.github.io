Target Audience

The target audience of the Thesunnymorning.com web site is described below:

  • Who:
    • It can be used by anyone who is looking to find out about the weather. Adults who are booking holidays to check the weather in a different country or who want to go on a fun day out.
  • Age:
    • 22-100.
  • Income:
    • Income levels will typicaly be between $50,000 and above annually.
  • Motivation:
    • Going out for vacations or traveling.
